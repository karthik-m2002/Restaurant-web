Our Story

Welcome to Hayiga Thinandi

Founded in 2010, Hayiga Thinandi has been serving exquisite cuisine in a warm and inviting atmosphere. Our passion for food and dedication to quality has made us a favorite dining destination.

Our chef brings over 15 years of culinary expertise, crafting dishes that blend traditional techniques with innovative flavors. We source our ingredients from local farmers and suppliers to ensure freshness and support our community.
